Query for Clinical Attributes

    cBioPortal uses standard (normalized) header for each clinical attribute. For each clinical attribute in your clinical file, please look up its matched NORMALIZED_COLUMN_HEADER by Table or Search Tab and use this NORMALIZED_COLUMN_HEADER as your column header.

    For more details, please see below.

  • NORMALIZED_COLUMN_HEADER: the header used for our system and clinical correlative excel templates
  • DISPLAY_NAME: the header shown in clinical table on the portal
  • DESCRIPTIONS: the clinical attribute descriptions when mouseover it
  • DATATYPE: the data format can be either text or number
  • ATTRIBUTE_TYPE: patient-based (ex. age or overall survival months) or sample-based (ex. specimen site)
